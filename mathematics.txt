arithematic Progression
-----------------------

Series : a, a+d, a+2d, a+3d, ......, a+(n-1)d
sum = (n/2)(2a + (n-1)d)
average : (first + last) /2


Geometric Progression:
-----------------------
Series : a,ar,ar^2, ar^3,.....
=> ar0,ar^1,ar^2,ar^3,....

nth term = ar^(n-1)

Sum of geometric Progression:

S = a(1-r^n)/(1-r)
